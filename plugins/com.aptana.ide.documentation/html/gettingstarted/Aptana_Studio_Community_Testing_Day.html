Aptana Studio Community Testing Day [edit]

We owe a debt of gratitude to Mozilla for this insipration. Community testing days are a chance for people to test a particular Aptana feature en masse and hopefully identify any remaining bugs as quickly as possible.

The next community testing day will predate the release of M8a to everyone.

The Aptana developers will be available on IRC to answer your questions, comments and whatever else you wish to discuss from 9AM to 5PM PST.

Getting Started

Thank you for helping out! Participating in a community testing day is very simple. Get your favorite IRC client:

  • Windows
  • MacOS
  • Linux
  • Firefox

Connect to chat.freenode.net -- Generally, this is done by typing /server chat.freenode.net and hitting Enter. Join the #aptana channel -- Generally, typing /join #aptana will get you into the channel.

What Build Should I Test?

  • Update Aptana to the "next" release. See Changing your update version type of Aptana
  • Create a new AJAX library project, including the "Debugger Samples" library. Start debugging with debug_tests.html

Channel Moderators

All Aptana developers/channel moderators will have "aptana" at the front of their name.

How do I file bugs?

Let one of the channel moderators know, and they may work with you to find out the exact cause. At this point, a moderator can file a new bug into the trac system.
