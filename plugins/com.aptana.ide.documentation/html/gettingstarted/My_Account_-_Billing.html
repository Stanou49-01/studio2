What

The Billing screen shows current credit cards on file for your account and which sites are associated with each.

Billing Tab
Add Credit Card form

How

x

Add a credit card

x Click the Add Credit Card button and fill out the form to add a new credit card.

Remove a credit card

x Click the Remove Card button at the right side of the row for that credit card. Note that cards associated with Aptana Cloud sites cannot be removed.

Updating a credit card

x When your card is about to expire or you want to change billing to a different card, use the Add Credit Card form then switch to the site's Settings tab and click the Use a different card link to select the newly-added card.

Getting to My Account - Billing

  1. Choose Open Aptana Home from the Help menu
  2. Click the 'My Account tab
  3. Click the Billing tab
